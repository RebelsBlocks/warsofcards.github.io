🎴 What is Wars of Cards?

Wars of Cards is a hub for simple, exciting card games on NEAR. The goal is to create a space where players can compete daily, earn tokens, and co-create the community through feedback and engagement.

Key Features

  • Blockchain-Based Gaming — All games run on the NEAR blockchain for transparency and security
  • Play Money System — Use CARDS tokens for betting without real money risk
  • Multiplayer Experience — Play with other users in real-time
  • Community Driven — Your feedback shapes the future of the platform
