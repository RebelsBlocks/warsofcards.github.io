Introduction

Wars of Cards is a multiplayer card gaming platform delivering a new paradigm in Web3 gaming — instant multiplayer interactions while securing all financial transactions fully on-chain (bets, payouts, token transfers).

The game is free to play. All you need is:

  • an active NEAR wallet
  • a stable internet connection

You can learn more about NEAR Wallets at learnnear.club

1 NEAR token will be sufficient for you to play thousands of rounds in Wars Of Cards.

To buy NEAR, you'll use a cryptocurrency exchange or platform like Binance, Coinbase, OKX, or MoonPay, by creating an account, verifying your identity (KYC), linking a payment method (card, bank), selecting NEAR, entering the amount, and confirming the purchase to receive it in your wallet.
