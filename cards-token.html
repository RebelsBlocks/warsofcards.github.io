CARDS Token

Token Contract

ft.warsofcards.near

Token Mechanics

Tokens are obtained via:

  • Daily claims
  • Pack purchases
  • Blackjack winnings

Tokens are burned when:

  • Placing bets
  • Doubling down
  • Splitting hands

Supply Model

Tracked values:

  • total_supply
  • total_minted
  • total_burned

Circulating supply = total_minted − total_burned

Supply dynamically changes with player activity.

Treasury

Revenue sources:

  1. Transfer fees — 7% on every token transfer (total_fees_collected)
  2. Pack purchases — 100% of NEAR deposits

Treasury accumulates all protocol revenue.

Treasury NEAR account: treasury.warsofcards.near
