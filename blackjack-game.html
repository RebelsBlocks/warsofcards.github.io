Real-Time Results

See results instantly.

Game Features

  • Up to 3 players per table
  • All bets and payouts are recorded on NEAR
  • Standard Blackjack Rules
  • Server-authoritative game logic

Game Actions

  • Hit — Draw another card to increase your hand value
  • Stand — Keep your current hand and end your turn
  • Double Down — Double your bet and receive exactly one more card
  • Split — If you have two cards of the same value, split them into two separate hands

Payouts

  • Blackjack — 3:2 payout (1.5× your play money bet)
  • Win — 1:1 payout (2× your play money bet)
  • Push — Return your original bet
  • Bust — Lose your bet
💡 Tip

Dealer stands on 17 or higher and hits on 16 or lower!
