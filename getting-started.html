Step 2: Connect to Blackjack and Start Playing

1

Visit Blackjack

Go to https://blackjack.warsofcards.online/

2

Connect Wallet

Click "Connect Wallet" and choose your wallet. Approve the connection request.

3

Deposit Storage

You'll need to deposit 0.0123 NEAR tokens to the contract to register your account. This ensures your interactions can be processed on-chain.

4

Claim Daily CARDS

After successful deposit, claim your daily 100 CARDS tokens, which will be used for betting.

5

Click an empty seat (1, 2, or 3) to join the table.

6

Place Your Bet

Choose how many play money tokens to wager on your hand.

7

Play!

Make your Hit / Stand / Split / Double decisions. Winnings are added automatically.
