Step 1: Get a NEAR Wallet

Only Meteor Wallet, MyNearWallet, and Intear Wallet are supported.

These wallets use Function Call Access Keys, enabling automatic transaction signing for seamless gameplay.

Never share your wallet seed phrase with anyone. Your seed phrase is the only way to recover your wallet account.
