Getting Started

Step 1: Login via NEAR Wallet

1.1 Get a NEAR Wallet

⚠️ Security Notice
Wars of Cards will never ask for your seed phrase or private keys. Never share them with anyone. You wouldn't give a stranger the keys to your home.
Simplest comparison:

  • Wallet address = bank account number (you can give to anyone)
  • Private key/seeds phrases = PIN + bank password (never show anyone)

Wallets:

  • Meteor Wallet → https://wallet.meteorwallet.app/
  • MyNearWallet → https://app.mynearwallet.com/ (VPN not supported)
  • Intear Wallet → https://wallet.intear.tech/

You can learn more about NEAR Wallets at learnnear.club

While all NEAR wallets support Function Call Access Keys for automatic transaction signing, only three provide a true plug-and-play experience suitable for multiplayer gaming.

1 NEAR token will be sufficient for you to play thousands of rounds in Wars Of Cards.

To buy NEAR, you'll use a cryptocurrency exchange or platform like Binance, Coinbase, OKX, or MoonPay, by creating an account, verifying your identity (KYC), linking a payment method (card, bank), selecting NEAR, entering the amount, and confirming the purchase to receive it in your wallet.

Note: Claiming tokens, depositing, and withdrawing still require explicit user signatures.

1.2 Connect to the Blackjack Lobby

  1. Visit: https://blackjack.warsofcards.online/
  2. Click Enter The Forest
  3. Choose your wallet and sign the transaction adding a limited Function Call Key

Recommended browsers: Chrome or Firefox for best gameplay experience.

Step 2: Lobby

2.1 Deposit Storage

To register your account:

  • 0.0123 NEAR — Blackjack contract storage
  • 0.0013 NEAR — Token contract storage

2.2 Claim Daily CARDS

Before playing Blackjack, you must claim CARDS tokens.

  • 50 CARDS every 24 hours
  • Minimum bet: 10 CARDS → at least 5 rounds daily even with bad luck
  • Daily claim amount is modifiable by the contract owner

2.3 Purchase CARDS

You can purchase token packs directly from the token contract:

Pack CARDS Cost
First 100 1 NEAR
Second 320 3 NEAR
Third 600 5 NEAR

Pack pricing and sizes can be modified by the contract owner.

2.4 Take a Seat or Take a Look

  • Join Battle — Join the table (up to 3 players)
  • Observe — Spectate if all seats are occupied

Step 3: Table

3.1 Place Your Bet

Available bet sizes:

  • 10 CARDS
  • 30 CARDS
  • 50 CARDS
  • 100 CARDS

3.2 Make Your Move

  • Hit / Stand → off-chain (instant results)
  • Split / Double → on-chain (tokens burned via supply reduction)

Higher risk actions unlock higher rewards.

All winnings are automatically credited to your in-contract balance.

3.3 Chat

Chat with seated players or spectators during live gameplay.
