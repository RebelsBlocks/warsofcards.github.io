üöÄ Getting Started - Step by Step

Step 1: Get a NEAR Wallet

  1. Go to Meteor Wallet or your preferred NEAR wallet
  2. Create a new account or import an existing one
  3. Write down your seed phrase safely (never share it!)
  4. Fund your wallet with some NEAR tokens

Step 2: Visit Wars of Cards

  1. Go to the Wars of Cards website
  2. Click "Connect Wallet"
  3. Select your wallet and approve the connection
  4. You should see your balance and available features

Step 3: Get Card Tokens

  1. Daily Claim: Click "Claim Daily" to get free play tokens
  2. Buy More: If you want more tokens, click "Buy Cards" and spend NEAR
  3. Check Balance: See how many play tokens you have available

Step 4: Play Blackjack

  1. Join a Table: Click on an empty seat (1, 2, or 3)
  2. Place a Bet: Choose how many play tokens to bet
  3. Play Your Hand: Make decisions when it's your turn
  4. Collect Winnings: If you win, tokens are added automatically

Step 5: Explore and Learn

  1. Chat with Vanessa: Ask questions about the game or Web3
  2. Try Different Strategies: Experiment with betting styles
  3. Join the Community: Connect with other players
  4. Learn About NEAR: Explore the broader NEAR ecosystem
