📋 What is Wars of Cards?

Wars of Cards is a Web3 entertainment platform built on NEAR Protocol featuring an AI assistant (Whisper), community chat, and interactive experiences. The platform serves as a demonstration of NEAR wallet integration and Web3 entertainment, with gaming features planned for future releases.

🎯 Current Status

This is currently an entertainment platform showcasing Web3 technology. Core features include AI chat assistance, community integration, and interactive experiences. Gaming features and token systems are in development.

🚀 Getting Started

Step 1: Get a NEAR Wallet

  1. Visit Meteor Wallet or your preferred NEAR wallet provider
  2. Create a new account or import an existing one
  3. Write down your seed phrase and store it safely
  4. Fund your wallet with some NEAR tokens (optional for basic features)
⚠️ Critical Security Step

Your seed phrase is the ONLY way to recover your wallet. Write it down on paper, never store it digitally, and NEVER share it with anyone.

Step 2: Visit Wars of Cards

  1. Navigate to the Wars of Cards website
  2. Click the "Connect Wallet" button
  3. Select your wallet from the list of options
  4. Approve the connection request in your wallet
  5. Verify that you can see your account information

Step 3: Explore the Platform

Once connected, you can explore these entertainment features:

Chat with Whisper

  1. Navigate to the Whisper chat interface
  2. Start a conversation or ask for help
  3. Enjoy interactive AI entertainment

Join the Community

  1. Visit the Community section
  2. Connect with other users through NEAR Social
  3. Participate in discussions and share experiences

Step 4: Enjoy and Explore

  • Chat with Whisper — Enjoy conversations and get platform assistance
  • Explore the Interface — Navigate through different entertainment sections

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is this an entertainment platform?

Yes! Wars of Cards is an entertainment platform showcasing NEAR blockchain integration. It features AI chat assistance, community features, and interactive experiences. Gaming features are planned for future releases.

Can I play games here?

Gaming features are not currently available on this platform. The game interface shows "Coming soon!" as it's under development. You can explore other entertainment features like AI chat and community discussions.

What can I do for entertainment?

You can connect your NEAR wallet, chat with the Whisper AI assistant, participate in community discussions through NEAR Social, and experience Web3 technology through interactive content.

Are there any tokens involved?

Currently, no token system is implemented. The platform focuses on wallet integration, AI assistance, and entertainment content. Token features are planned for future releases.

Which wallets are supported?

Wars of Cards supports all standard NEAR Protocol wallets, including Meteor Wallet, NEAR Wallet, and Sender Wallet.

How do I get NEAR tokens?

You can purchase NEAR tokens on cryptocurrency exchanges like Binance, Coinbase, or Kraken, then transfer them to your NEAR wallet. However, NEAR tokens are not required for basic platform features.

Is the platform secure?

Yes. The platform uses standard NEAR wallet integration for security. All wallet operations are handled by your wallet provider, and the platform never has access to your private keys.

What if I lose my seed phrase?

Unfortunately, if you lose your seed phrase, there is no way to recover your wallet. This is why it's critical to write it down and store it securely.

Can I use this on mobile?

Yes, Wars of Cards is designed to work on both desktop and mobile devices. Make sure you have a compatible NEAR wallet installed on your mobile device.

When will gaming features be available?

Gaming features are currently in development. Follow the platform updates or join the community discussions to stay informed about new releases.
