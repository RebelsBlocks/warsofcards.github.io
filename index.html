üöÄ Getting Started

Step 1: Get a NEAR Wallet

Only Meteor Wallet, MyNearWallet, and Intear Wallet are supported. These wallets use Function Call Access Keys, enabling automatic transaction signing for seamless gameplay.

üîó Get started with a NEAR wallet

‚ö†Ô∏è Critical Security Step

Never share your wallet seed phrase with anyone. Your seed phrase is the only way to recover your wallet account. Wars of Cards will never ask for your seed phrase or private keys.

Step 2: Connect to Blackjack and Start Playing

  1. Go to https://blackjack.warsofcards.online/
  2. Click "Connect Wallet."
  3. Choose your wallet and approve the connection request.
  4. Deposit Storage: You'll need to deposit 0.0123 NEAR tokens to the contract to register your account. This ensures your interactions can be processed on-chain.
  5. After a successful deposit, claim your daily 100 CARDS, which will be used for betting.
  6. Click an empty seat (1, 2, or 3).
  7. Choose how many play money tokens to wager.
  8. Make your Hit / Stand / Split / Double decisions.
  9. Winnings are added automatically.
