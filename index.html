üöÄ Getting Started

Step 1: Get a NEAR Wallet

  1. Visit Meteor Wallet or your preferred NEAR wallet provider
  2. Create a new account or import an existing one
  3. Write down your seed phrase and store it safely
  4. Fund your wallet with some NEAR tokens (available on exchanges)
‚ö†Ô∏è Critical Security Step

Your seed phrase is the ONLY way to recover your wallet. Write it down on paper, never store it digitally, and NEVER share it with anyone.

Step 2: Visit Wars of Cards

  1. Navigate to the Wars of Cards website
  2. Click the "Connect Wallet" button
  3. Select your wallet from the list of options
  4. Approve the connection request in your wallet
  5. Verify that you can see your balance and account information

Step 3: Get Card Tokens

You need play card tokens to place bets. There are two ways to get them:

Option 1: Daily Claim (Free)

  1. Look for the "Claim Daily" button on the dashboard
  2. Click it once per day to receive free play tokens
  3. Your balance will update automatically

Option 2: Purchase with NEAR

  1. Click the "Buy Cards" button
  2. Choose how many tokens you want to purchase
  3. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
  4. Wait for the blockchain confirmation (usually a few seconds)

Step 4: Play Blackjack

  1. Join a Table ‚Äî Click on an empty seat (1, 2, or 3)
  2. Place a Bet ‚Äî Choose how many play tokens to wager
  3. Play Your Hand ‚Äî Make hit/stand decisions when it's your turn
  4. Collect Winnings ‚Äî If you win, tokens are added to your balance automatically

Step 5: Explore and Learn

  • Chat with Vanessa ‚Äî Ask questions about the game or Web3 concepts
  • Try Different Strategies ‚Äî Experiment with various betting and playing styles
  • Join the Community ‚Äî Connect with other players in the chat
  • Learn About NEAR ‚Äî Explore the broader NEAR ecosystem

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is this real money gambling?

No, Wars of Cards uses play tokens for gaming. While you can purchase these tokens with NEAR, the primary focus is on learning blockchain technology and enjoying the game.

Which wallets are supported?

Wars of Cards supports all standard NEAR Protocol wallets, including Meteor Wallet, NEAR Wallet, and Sender Wallet.

How do I get NEAR tokens?

You can purchase NEAR tokens on cryptocurrency exchanges like Binance, Coinbase, or Kraken, then transfer them to your NEAR wallet.

Is the game fair?

Yes. The game logic runs on the server with provably fair algorithms. All transactions are recorded on the NEAR blockchain for transparency.

What if I lose my seed phrase?

Unfortunately, if you lose your seed phrase, there is no way to recover your wallet. This is why it's critical to write it down and store it securely.

Can I play on mobile?

Yes, Wars of Cards is designed to work on both desktop and mobile devices. Make sure you have a compatible NEAR wallet installed on your mobile device.
